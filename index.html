About Naruto

Naruto is a globally popular Japanese manga and anime franchise about naruto uzumaki,a mischievious but determined young ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the village leader(Hokage) to gain respect and acceptance,while navigating a world of shinobi battels,friendship,lossand overcoming prejudice as a vessel for a powerful demon fox. This series is created by Masashi Kishimoto.It is known for its action, comedy,themes of perserverance, and its vast universe of ninjas and special techniques.
